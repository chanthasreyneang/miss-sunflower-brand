About Miss Sunflower

Miss Sunflower is a Cambodian skincare brand founded by CEO Lai Souyiek. The brand offers quality beauty and personal care products ... Miss Sunflower focuses on using natural ingredients that are safe and effective for all skin types. The brand is committed to helping customers achieve healthy, radiant skin while maintaining affordability. Over the years, Miss Sunflower has gained the trust of many customers across Cambodia. With a passion for beauty and self-care, the company continues to innovate and expand its product line to meet modern skincare needs. Our mission is to empower confidence through high-quality skincare products that deliver real results.
